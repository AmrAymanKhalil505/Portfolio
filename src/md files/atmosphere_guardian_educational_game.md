## Project Overview

Atmosphere Guardian is a mobile educational game designed to teach children about air pollution and atmosphere protection. The player controls an environmental airplane that collects harmful gas clouds before they damage the atmosphere.

The game uses simple arcade interaction, visual feedback, health-based atmosphere protection, and upgrade mechanics to make environmental concepts easier for young players to understand. The instruction screens explain the core idea in Arabic, making the experience accessible for Arabic-speaking children.

## Learning Goal

The game teaches children that harmful emissions from cars, factories, and burning waste can pollute the air and weaken the atmosphere.

The player learns that protecting the environment can involve:

- collecting harmful gases
- reducing pollution
- recycling
- using filters
- switching to cleaner vehicles
- keeping the air clean

## Core Gameplay

The player controls a small airplane and moves through the level to collect harmful gas clouds.

Main loop:

1. Harmful gas clouds appear in the environment.
2. The player flies toward them and collects them.
3. The atmosphere health is protected when pollution is removed.
4. If too much harmful gas is left uncollected, atmosphere health decreases.
5. The player can collect upgrades to improve their ability to clean the air.
